About the Company

Founded in 2002, with the purpose of addressing industry needs related to the construction and maintenance of civil works in both public and private sectors. We have 23 years of experience, which has allowed us to build a passionate and committed professional team dedicated to meeting our clients' needs. We have a presence in the states of Querétaro, Michoacán, Hidalgo, Guanajuato, and the State of Mexico.

Vision

To be a technologically proactive and flexible company that guarantees sustainability in the construction of works in an integral manner, respecting clients’ needs and expectations.

Mission

A company dedicated to comprehensive construction in both public and private sectors, attentive to client needs and current technical, safety, health, and environmental regulations.

Our Values

Policies

Quality Policy

To meet and satisfy customer needs while striving to exceed expectations in the construction of public and private works; directing and executing the activities of our technical and human resources with passion, fostering continuous improvement in processes, services, technology, and staff training.

Environmental Policy

We recognize our responsibility, protection, and care of the environment. We adopt sustainability principles with the firm objective of being a socially responsible company, acting in accordance with environmental legislation, standards, and regulations.

Safety Policy
